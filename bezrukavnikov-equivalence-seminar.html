Seminar on Bezrukavnikov's equivalence

The goal is to understand a theorem of Bezrukavnikov giving geometric realizations of affine Hecke algebras. You can find a program for the seminar here.
Organizers: Bhargav Bhatt, Jacob Lurie, Kenta Suzuki, Mingjia Zhang
When? Fridays 2-4PM at Simonyi Hall 101

Schedule

  • January 30 (Kenta). Organization and Introduction Notes
  • February 6 (Yuta). Representation-theoretic background Notes
  • February 13. Geometry of affine Grassmannian and flag varieties
  • Febuary 20 (Longke). Geometric Satake I: The Satake category
  • Febuary 27 (Elias). Geometric Satake II: The commutativity constraint
  • March 6 (Jacob). Geometric Satake III: Constant term functors
  • March 13 (Xingzhu). Geometric Satake IV: Identification of the dual group
  • March 20 (Quanlin). Geometric Satake V: Derived Satake
  • March 27 (Mingjia). Springer and Steinberg varieties
  • April 3 (Naomi). Iwahori Hecke algebra and their representations
  • April 10 (Bhargav). Gaitsgory's central functor I: Construction
  • April 17 (Longke). Gaitsgory's central functor II: Centrality
  • April 24 (Xingzhu). Wakimoto sheaves and Wakimoto filtration
  • May 1 (Michael). Arkhipov-Bezrukavnikov Equivalence I: Construction
  • May 4 (Guido). Arkhipov-Bezrukavnikov Equivalence II: Whittaker-averaging
  • May 8 (Kenta). Arkhipov-Bezrukavnikov Equivalence III: Proof
